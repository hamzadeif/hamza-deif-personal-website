Halal Blackjack

This is a Halal version of Blackjack, so no money is involved! Read the rules below.

Cards:

Sum:

You start off with two random cards, and their sum will be shown.

You will have the option to grab a new card.

The closer your sum gets to 21, the better. If you hit 21 exaclty, then you've got Blackjack!

But if it's over 21, you lose!
